Episode 1: The Nautiloid Crash

Posted April 23, 2025

I awaken to the wet hiss of alien flesh. My head throbs — a parasite squirming in my skull. I'm on a mind flayer ship, chained by fate to strangers and horrors alike. The sky outside? Shattered by war between Githyanki riders and eldritch monstrosities.

The nautiloid lurches, crashing through realms and realities before slamming into Faerûn. I crawl from the wreckage, dazed, but free. Almost. Something stirs inside me — a hunger. Magic? Mutation? Time will tell.

This is the first page of my story. I do not yet know who I will become — hero, villain, or something in between. But I will shape my path with steel, spell, and choice.
